Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a habit?

From the main screen, tap Check in to start your daily check-in. You can mark each habit as Done or Skipped.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings, then tap Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

How do I sync across devices?

Sign in with your Apple ID in Settings, then Account. Your data will sync via iCloud across all your devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then LifeBlocks, then Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to iOS Settings, Apple ID, iCloud, Manage Storage, LifeBlocks, Delete Data.
